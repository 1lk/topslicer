  • 1. Send your files

    Raster artwork, or bitmaps, is any digital art composed of horizontal and vertical rows of pixels. As a result, when raster images are enlarged, the image quality diminishes significantly. Typical raster file types include .jpg, .gif, .png, .tif, .psd and .bmp

  • 2. Review the quotation

    If you are happy with the quote, we will promptly start hand tracing your image upon receiving payment. If needed, revisions can be made to fix any errors with the design. Let us know if you're not happy, we will make it right!

  • 3. Receive your vectors

    Vector artwork is digital art composed of mathematical lines and curves. As a result, vector images can be reduced or enlarged in size indefinitely, without any loss to image quality. Typical vector file types include .ai, .pdf, .eps, and .svg

Where are vectors used?

Vector files are preferred for commercial & specialty printers (including Ads, Business Cards, Brochures, Doorhangers, Flyers, Menus, Newsletters, Postcards, Posters, Product Packaging, Stationery), cut-ready art for signs, banners, vinyls, engravers, logo designs, infographics, illustrations, graphics on the web and more.

How do I get a quote?

Simply click on the "Upload an Image" button. Fill out the contact and order info. Once you have uploaded the file you can click "Submit" and the quote request will be immediately processed. You will be contacted via email with a quote and can either agree to process the order, or decline. You may also simply not reply.

How is payment handled?

Payments are handled via Bitcoin or Paypal. We do not process credit cards or bank transfers. Payments must be made before custom vector hand tracing can begin on the artwork. All good solutions require an investment in time, effort, and energy to create, a well designed vector artwork should not be considered any different.

When will I get my files?

99% of our jobs are done in less than 24 hours. Most of them are done much sooner than that. However, if the artwork is detailed, we will send you a quote for the turnaround time before starting on your artwork. Orders received Friday through Sunday may not start until Monday unless agreed otherwise by both parties.
